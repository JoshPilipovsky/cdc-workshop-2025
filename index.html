About the Workshop

Recent advances in stochastic control theory have opened new avenues for addressing uncertainty in complex dynamical systems. This full‑day workshop brings together leading, early‑career and student researchers from the communities of stochastic control, uncertainty quantification and optimization to explore cutting‑edge methodologies for planning and controlling systems under uncertainty. By highlighting connections between these disciplines, we aim to develop practical algorithms that can handle the challenges of real‑world applications, particularly in aerospace and autonomous systems.

Throughout the day, attendees will be exposed to a suite of different approaches and methodologies designed to enable safe and robust trajectory planning and control in uncertain environments. Speakers will showcase theory and implementations, and the organizers will consolidate the material in a companion open source Python package so that ideas presented at the workshop are easy to adopt and extend.

Workshop Tracks

Stochastic MPC & Data‑Driven Control

Control methods that explicitly incorporate constraints and risk‑aware planning into trajectory optimisation.

  • Stochastic Predictive Control: planning in a receding horizon framework where the objective and constraints are formulated probabilistically, ensuring stability and feasibility under uncertainty via convex and stochastic optimisation.
  • Data‑Driven & Distributionally Robust Control: synthesising control laws directly from noisy data and exploiting trajectory data to represent systems without explicit identification. Distributional robustness protects against ambiguity in the underlying data.

Control of Distributions

Techniques aimed at steering probability distributions and handling model uncertainty via distributional methods.

  • Optimal Transport: tools for quantifying distances between probability measures and designing controllers that steer systems between distributions or are robust to uncertainty in the underlying measures.
  • Density Steering: shaping the full state distribution (not just the mean), encompassing covariance steering and connections to Schrödinger bridges and optimal transport.

Stochastic Safety & Probabilistic Guarantees

Methods for ensuring system safety via safety filters and optimal control, and verifying reachability under stochastic disturbances.

  • Stochastic Reachability: computing the probability that a stochastic system reaches or avoids particular sets using dynamic programming or Hamilton–Jacobi–Bellman equations, with applications to control and safety filters.
  • Stochastic Control Barrier Functions: extending deterministic barrier functions to guarantee safety with high probability by ensuring safe sets remain forward invariant under stochastic dynamics.

Applications in Aerospace

Implementation and validation of theoretical methods in complex, uncertain environments.

  • Aerospace Applications: showcasing algorithms that combine theory and practice on real‑world problems such as low‑thrust cislunar and interplanetary guidance and turbulence‑affected quadrotor trajectory optimisation.

Outreach & Engagement

The workshop fosters dissemination of ideas between researchers from academia and industry across North America and Europe. Our objectives are twofold: to aid practitioners in realising tools from academia, and to provide practical examples—particularly in aerospace—that motivate fundamental research.

To enhance accessibility, the organisers are preparing an open-source Python package that implements the approaches presented by the speakers. We hope that this lowers the barrier to entry for newcomers and encourages attendees to experiment directly with the techniques and engage in deeper theoretical discussions.

The spotlight sessions are specifically designed to provide opportunities for Ph.D. students and postdoctoral fellows to present their work, engage with experts and receive feedback. We believe that fostering interaction with new researchers will nurture the next generation of experts in stochastic planning and control.
